About Us

We at HandleHandler know how frustrating it can be to never have your dream handle.

Having to add uneccessary numbers to the end can really sour your brand or make you feel disconnected from what you wish to present yourself as.

If you reached out to the person with the handle you want, would they be willing to give it? Do they even use the platform anymore??

So we asked ourselves, what if there was a hub for people looking to trade their usernames that makes it easier to come in contact with and manage the transfer? Thus, HandleHandler was born.

We give people a place to find handles as well as a place to list their own. Don't feel bad about the 99 at the end of your own anymore - you have a chance to upgrade now!
